Who We Are

Dedicated to restoring the gift of sound and speech in Vadakara.

Compassionate Care Since 2026

Life Audiology And Speech Therapy Center was founded with a single mission: to provide world-class communication healthcare that is accessible to everyone. We believe that hearing and speech are the foundation of human connection.


Our facility near Cee Yam Hospital is equipped with the latest diagnostic tools, ensuring that every patient—from newborns to senior citizens—receives a precise diagnosis and a personalized treatment plan.

Our Commitment

"We don't just treat symptoms; we empower individuals to communicate with confidence and live their lives to the fullest."

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and evidence-based audiology and speech services using state-of-the-art technology.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in Kerala for communication disorders, fostering a world where no one is limited by hearing or speech barriers.
