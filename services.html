Our Specialized Services

World-class diagnostic and rehabilitative care in Vadakara.

Hearing Assessment

We use gold-standard clinical equipment to ensure the most accurate results for all age groups.

Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA)

The standard behavioral test to measure hearing thresholds across different frequencies.

Speech Audiometry

Tests how well you can hear and understand speech in quiet and noisy environments.

Immittance Audiometry

Assessing middle ear function and reflex pathways to detect fluid or pressure issues.

OAE Screening

Otoacoustic Emissions test, essential for newborn hearing screening and inner ear health.

Speech & Language Therapy

Professional support for misarticulation, stammering, and delayed speech development.

Hearing Aid Solutions

Digital hearing aid trials, precision fitting, and lifetime service support.

AVT & Tinnitus Management
